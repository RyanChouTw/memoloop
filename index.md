## About

MemoLoop is a free Android app designed to help you learn English through **vocabulary flashcards** and **listening comprehension** exercises. With daily quizzes, achievement rewards, and a streak system, MemoLoop keeps you motivated to practice every day.

## Features

### 📚 Vocabulary Review
Review 30 randomized flashcards per session. Each card includes the English word, Chinese definition, part of speech, and an example sentence. Rate your confidence to control when each card reappears.

### 🎧 Listening Comprehension
Listen to short English stories read aloud and answer comprehension questions. Adjust the speech speed to match your level — from slow (0.5x) to normal (1x). Tap to reveal the transcript and look up unfamiliar words with built-in translate and copy actions.

### 📝 Daily Quiz
Challenge yourself with a daily vocabulary quiz (10 questions) and listening quiz (5 stories). Score 100% to earn points — the higher your streak, the more points you earn.

### 🏆 Achievements & Streaks
Build a daily practice streak and unlock five achievement tiers: Rookie → Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum. Keep the streak alive for 30 days to become a Vocabulary Legend.

### 📊 Progress Tracking
View your learning history on a monthly calendar. Track your active days, total review time, quiz results, and accumulated points.

## Three Difficulty Levels

| Level | Vocabulary | Listening Stories |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| Beginner | 800 words | 100 stories |
| Advanced | 2,500 words | 100 stories |
| Professional | 5,000+ words | 100 stories |
